School

    All-Russian Children Center “Okean” operates within the framework of holistic educational process in developing and implementing thematic programs.

    The main objective of the school is to create conditions for mastering the educational programs according to the students’ educational and training background.

    “Okean”’s school is the school of active learning without any homework. A variety of learning methods and activities during classes, multidisciplinary educational units, training sessions, problem laboratories and discussion panel boards enable students to establish their own individual educational paths.

    Over the years, a team of like-minded, highly-qualified, and creative faculty has been formed. Among them, there are both experienced and young teachers, the finalists of professional excellence contests, and educators with awarded titles, like the “Honorary Teacher” and the “Honorary Worker of General Education of the Russian Federation”.

    The faculty implements state programs for the basic and general secondary education. They design and implement various educational methods focused on supporting each student in their acquisition of the new FSES (Federal State Educational Standards) in major subjects. According to the statute of FSFEI ARCC “Okean” and based on the activities conducted on campus, such disciplines as “Art”, “Technology”, “Physical Education” and “Safety Fundamentals” are included into the schedule of supplementary education for children.

    One of the primary foci of the educational process is to organize and conduct research and project activities among students of the 6-11 grades.

    Within the framework of the development program 2020 for the Federal State-Funded Educational Institution “Okean,” a research laboratory has been created with the purpose of running cross-disciplinary, hands-on sessions in Natural Science. The Language Research Laboratory is intended for conducting all types of academic work, like interactive lectures and seminars, hands-on and lab sessions in a wide range of subjects in the liberal arts.

    The school classrooms are equipped with modern technology (interactive electronic boards, projectors, computers, Physics and Chemistry laboratories), various teaching materials and textbooks, and extra reference materials. Foreign language classrooms are equipped with lingaphone equipment.

    All of that enables the teaching process to be intensive and diverse, and makes learning interesting, exciting and joyful!
