“Brigantina” Division

    A snow white beauty “Brigantine” is the first year-round division at “Okean”. It started its operation in 1983. It overlooks the Sea of Japan and the Cape Engelm.

    “Brigantine” provides all the facilities for innovative education and proper rest. There are washrooms, a hundred comfortable living quarters for children (cabins), showers, a spacious canteen, a medical department, a Concert Hall for 800 seats, a school, crafts workshops, a library with a reading hall and electronic resources, IT zones where children can communicate with their relatives and search for relevant information online. “Okean” locates its Media Center here, as well as the children’s entertainment complex. The latter consists of a sports bar “Olymp” and a kiosk “Okesha’s Goods” where cashiers, consultants, and administrators are interns, the very students who participate in the economic educational programs. The kiosk has a wide assortment of original souvenirs and everyday articles with “Okean” logo.

    Across from the “Brigantine,” there is an open-air amphitheater with a seating capacity for 2000 people. It hosts the largest camp activities. The jewel of the construction is the 15-metre musical fountain located in the center of the amphitheater.

    The division hosts up to 540 students each session. They participate in innovative educational programs such as “My Federation”, “Russian Intellect”, “Children of the World”, “Science. Technology. Progress”, “Modern Art Forum”, “Business-Express”, “The Land of Childhood,” and many others.
