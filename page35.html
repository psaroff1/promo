Educational environment “Etnokrug”

     Russia is the country, which has united huge territories and a great number of the people. Each of it is unique by the nature and all of them are an integral part of Russian culture.

    All the year round children of different nationalities gather in the City of childhood. Here they share traditions and legends of their regions through the informal communication. Thanks to unique experience of “Okean” in organization of additional education and implementation of the Program of development 2014-2020 will appear “Etnokrug” in the Center in 2018. It is innovative environment, which combine ethnic images and modern educational technologies. “Etnokrug” will become tiny reflection of our huge and multicultural country. Each child who came here will be able to see wealth and variety of the Russian Federation, visually present beauty and singularity of the small homeland, to make a personal contribution in creation of its image.

    There will be allocated six zones-modules on the territory of multifunctional educational platform. Five zones will be allocated by cultural geographically principle: European, Caucasian, Ural, Siberian, Far East areas. The sixth zone is multicultural and uniting.

       European module will be devoted to the people who lives in west part of Russia, which characterized by complex alloy of the ethnic phenomena and processes. Acquaintance to this zone can help understand beginnings of national outlook.

    Caucasian module plays an important role in politics and culture of Russia. These region is connected with military valor and feats in the national worldview. Ural module is organically connects the past, present and future of the state's economic power. Siberian module is the discovery of Asian Russia. Far East region allows not only meet with the farthest region for Russians but to strengthen the frontier of the state on ideological level.

    The sixth module is necessary like connecting link which unites various regions and people at single mental field of the state. This place will become a symbol of multiculturalism, which shows equality and mutual respect.
