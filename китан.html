The “Kityonok-Tigryonok” Summer Complex

   While roaming around the campus of “Okean”, one may miss out most of the cozy cottages of the summer complex. Hidden from the rain and the sun among the large trees, the latter has a special atmosphere for children’s rest, the seashore with golden sand being only 30 to 40 meters away.

    “Kityonok-Tigryonok” (“The Baby Whale” and “The Baby Tiger”) complex has comfortable summer cottages equipped with air conditioners, a canteen, entertainment centers, amenity premises, medical facilities, sports grounds, bonfire grounds, unit grounds and a fountain.

    This summer complex can host up to 500 children at the same time. It offers traditional sessions, like “Safe Roads”, “Strong People”, “Show Maker”, an ecological forum “Breathe, the Earth!” along with other events devoted to protecting our planet’s endangered species.
